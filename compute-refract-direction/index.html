Snell’s Law

首先介绍下菲涅尔定律，光入射到不同介质的界面上会发生反射和折射。其中入射光和折射光位于同一平面，且满足以下公式：

$$\eta{_1} \sin(\theta_1) = \eta{_2}\sin(\theta{_2})$$

$$\frac{\eta_1}{\eta_2} = \frac{\sin({\theta_2})}{\sin({\theta_1})}$$

入射光线L可以表示为:$$(-\sin(\theta_1) , \cos(\theta_1))$$

法线$$n$$表示为:$$(0 , 1)$$

折射光线T可以表示为:$$(\sin(\theta_2) , -\cos(\theta_2))$$

设两个未知数：x , y , 使得以下式子成立:

$$\overrightarrow{T} = x \overrightarrow{L} + y \overrightarrow{n}$$

因此:

$$
\begin{equation}
\begin{cases}
\sin(\theta_2) = -\sin(\theta_1) x + 0 y \
-\cos(\theta_2) = \cos(\theta_1) x + 1 y
\end{cases}
\end{equation}
$$

$$
\begin{equation}
\begin{cases}
x = -\frac{\sin(\theta_2)}{\sin(\theta_1)} \
y = \cos(\theta_1) * \frac{\sin(\theta_2)}{\sin(\theta_1)} - \cos(\theta_2)
\end{cases}
\end{equation}
$$

因此：

$$\overrightarrow{T} = -\frac{\sin(\theta_2)}{\sin(\theta_1)} \overrightarrow{L} + (\cos(\theta_1) \frac{\sin(\theta_2)}{\sin(\theta_1)} - \cos(\theta_2)) * \overrightarrow{n}$$

$$\because$$
$$\cos(\theta_2) = \sqrt{1 - \sin^2(\theta_2)} = \sqrt{1 - \frac{\sin^2(\theta_2)}{\sin^2(\theta_1)}(1 - \cos^2(\theta_1))} = \sqrt{1 - \frac{\sin^2(\theta_2)}{\sin^2(\theta_1)}(1 - (\overrightarrow{L} \cdot \overrightarrow{n})^2)}$$

$$\overrightarrow{T} = -\frac{\sin(\theta_2)}{\sin(\theta_1)} \overrightarrow{L} + (\cos(\theta_1) \frac{\sin(\theta_2)}{\sin(\theta_1)} - \sqrt{1 - \frac{\sin^2(\theta_2)}{\sin^2(\theta_1)}(1 - (\overrightarrow{L} \cdot \overrightarrow{n})^2)}) * \overrightarrow{n}$$

$$\overrightarrow{T} = -\frac{\eta_1}{\eta_2} * \overrightarrow{L} + \overrightarrow{n}(\frac{\eta_1}{\eta_2}(\overrightarrow{L} \cdot \overrightarrow{n}) - \sqrt{1 - \frac{\eta_1^2}{\eta_2^2}(1 - (\overrightarrow{L} \cdot \overrightarrow{n})^2)})$$
